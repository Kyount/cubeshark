CUBESHARK MUSIC & SOUND DESIGN

Cubeshark is the musical alias of Kyle Yount, an electronic producer, composer, and sound designer. Having grown up in the Pacific Northwest, he is influenced by technology and nature, exhibiting a unique melodic and emotionally expressive style. He is a long-time user of Propellerheads Reason to produce music and has a certification in Sound Design from Berklee College of music in said program. Kyle produces music not only for listening but also for other media such as film and video games. The list on this site is a curated selection of his work a full list including older and unfinished pieces can be found on his SoundCloud page.
